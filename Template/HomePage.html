TOWARDS PAINLESS
STEPS

At Qadam, we believe every child deserves the chance to walk confidently toward a brighter future. We provide children in need with proper footwear, an essential yet often overlooked necessity that impacts their health, education, and well-being. Lack of shoes can lead to injuries, infections, and long-term health issues, making everyday activities like walking to school or playing difficult. By offering quality shoes, we help protect their feet and give them the dignity and confidence they deserve.

AWARENESS
AND
EDUCATION

Proper footwear also plays a critical role in education. For children in underserved communities, shoes are often a prerequisite for attending school. Without them, many children miss out on valuable learning opportunities, perpetuating cycles of poverty.

Our collection drives are at the heart of this mission, bringing neighbourhoods, societies, and apartment complexes together to make a tangible difference in the lives of children in need. By setting up donation bins and hosting weekend events, we make it easy for individuals to contribute.

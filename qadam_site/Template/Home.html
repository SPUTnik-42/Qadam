TOWARDS PAINLESS STEPS

Every step a child takes should be filled with comfort and dignity. At Qadam, we believe that a pair of shoes is more than just footwear – it's a gateway to education, play, and childhood joy. Since 2018, we've provided over 100,000 pairs of quality shoes to children in need across 15 countries. Our community-based approach ensures that each donation directly reaches the children who need it most. Through partnerships with local schools and organizations, we're walking towards a future where no child has to go barefoot.
